The week in brief
The dominant throughline this week is the sheer scale of capital mobilizing around AI infrastructure and autonomous systems, with sovereign and corporate actors alike making bets measured in the tens of billions — MGX's $49 billion AI fund, ByteDance's $20 billion loan and $39 billion Brazil commitment, and Together AI's $800 million raise collectively signal that the private-market AI buildout is entering a phase where only balance-sheet giants can compete at the frontier. The one thing to watch is whether ByteDance's parallel capital offensives across geographies and asset classes begin to attract regulatory scrutiny that could redefine how Western LPs and sovereigns engage with Chinese-linked AI infrastructure at this scale.
